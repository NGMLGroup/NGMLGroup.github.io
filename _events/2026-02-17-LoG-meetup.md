**What is LoG?** The Learning on Graphs (LoG) Conference is a premier annual research venue dedicated to machine learning on graphs and geometry. It brings together the brightest minds to discuss the latest advancements in Graph Neural Networks, geometric deep learning, and their applications.

**Why join the local meetup?** We are bringing the global conference experience to Troms√∏! We will be hosting Keynote lectures, coding sessions, tutorials and a small poster session. It will be the perfect occasion to connect with many local researchers!

**Who is this for?** This event is open to everybody! Whether you are a PhD candidate, a professor, an industry practitioner, or a curious Master's student, you are warmly invited. If you are a student interested in AI, this is a fantastic opportunity to see what state-of-the-art research looks like.

üçï Did we mention **pizza**? Stick around! We will be serving pizza at the end of the day to fuel the networking and discussions.
